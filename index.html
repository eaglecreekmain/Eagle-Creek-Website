Experience You Can Build On

Eagle Creek Construction was founded on a simple principle: do the work right, treat people fairly, and stand behind what you build. The owner built this company from the ground up — starting with a truck, a toolbox, and a commitment to never cutting corners.

"I started Eagle Creek because I saw too many people get burned by contractors who overpromised and underdelivered. We say what we'll do, and we do what we say."

With over 15 years of hands-on construction experience, the owner brings deep expertise across general contracting, structural repair, and steel building construction. Having worked in the trades from an early age, they developed a craftsman's eye for detail and a foreman's instinct for keeping projects on track.

Eagle Creek's specialty in general contracting grew from years of managing complex multi-trade projects — coordinating crews, navigating permit systems, and delivering results that exceed expectations. The steel building division was added in response to growing regional demand for durable, cost-effective structures across agricultural, commercial, and industrial sectors.

When not on a job site, the owner is invested in the community — coaching youth sports, supporting local businesses, and mentoring the next generation of builders. Eagle Creek isn't just a construction company. It's a reflection of how this owner has lived and worked for their entire career.
